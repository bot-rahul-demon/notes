EXHAUSTIVE TOPIC & SUBTOPIC BREAKDOWN | HIGH-EMPHASIS SUBJECT

difficulty (easier than GATE). Emphasis on definitions, ER/relational concepts,
SQL output, normalization up to BCNF, and standard procedures. Items tagged
"(advanced - awareness)" are low priority. Cover every leaf point; none left.
