EXHAUSTIVE TOPIC & SUBTOPIC BREAKDOWN | HIGH-EMPHASIS SUBJECT

Exam note: CIL MT is an objective (MCQ) computer-based test, conceptual-to-moderate
difficulty (easier than GATE). Focus on recognizing algorithms, their steps,
complexities, and applying standard results/formulas. Deep proofs are NOT required;
items tagged "(advanced - awareness)" are low priority. Cover every leaf point.
